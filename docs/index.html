Arke.app (work in progress)

(arke.app) - (GitHub)

Decentralized, server-less, point to point encrypted messenger app: No servers; No sniffing; No control; No influence.

Arke (Greek: Ἄρκη Arkē) was a daughter of Thaumas. During the Titanomachy, she betrayed the Olympian gods and joined the Titans against them; she became the messenger for the Titans, while her fraternal twin sister Iris was on the opposite side and became the messenger of the Olympian Gods.

arke.app is an effort to oppose the Olympian Gods with their worldwide server clusters, big data, artificial intelligence and give power back to the Titans, to communicate freely once more.
